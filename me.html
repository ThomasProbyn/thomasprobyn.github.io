About Me

I am Tom, I’m 21 years old and living in Newcastle Upon Tyne. I try to find the greatest joy I possibly can in things which don’t cost myself, other people, or the planet, and this site acts as a bit of a shrine to that.

I’m interested in many most things, some of which have a page on the website. I’m a radio amateur, and I enjoy writing songs and recording music. I’m a recovering former university student, where I studied UX (User Experience) Design. I’ve written a lot about the technical side of design, such as descisions around how things like web technologies get implemented, and programming is something I’ve been doing since I was very, very little indeed!

You’ve had the facts, but the best way to learn about me as a person it to have a poke around this site! Two good places to start are my /tags page, which lists all the topics I’ve written blogs about. Click a topic to find all the blogs about it. Alternatively, try the /everything page, which is a list of all of the top-level pages on the entire site!

Please do explore the website, and if you feel so compelled, try contacting me!
