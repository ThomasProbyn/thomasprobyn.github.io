Musical Grid In NLEs

23rd December 2024

I sometimes wish there was a way to switch NLEs like Final Cut and Motion over to using musical time (letting you set a BPM/time sig and snap clips to beats and bars). Nearly every DAW can display film time (feet and frames), so why can’t Final Cut do the same for musical time? I feel it would be helpful for music video editing.
