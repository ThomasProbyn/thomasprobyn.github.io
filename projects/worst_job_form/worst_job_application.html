The world’s worst job application form

I’m currently in the stage of applying for jobs after having left university. On the whole, it’s a mostly plesant experience, but over the past month or two, I have come across a few web application forms which have been utterly shockingly awful (not to mention some pretty flagrant GDPR/protected characteristics violations in my view). The following page was inspired by r/BadUIBattles on Reddit.com, but nearly every element here is based on something I have actually seen when applying for a real world job, believe it or not.

NB - This page is a silly joke. No data is collected at all from this form, it’s just a piece of art toy with no backend. Everything stays entirely within your browser. There is no JavaScript, it’s just a pure HTML page so enter into it what you want!

Job Application Form

Preface

  1. It is recommended that you view this page on Internet Explorer, as parts of this page do not render well in other browsers. Netscape Navigator or Lynx will also do, but any browser supporting HTML5 may be too modern for this page.

  2. YOU MUST NEVER USE THE BACK BUTTON IN YOUR BROWSER OR REFRESH WHILST ON THIS PAGE, PLEASE USE THE BUTTONS ON THE FORM ONLY

  3. Please copy and paste then save your answers to each question in a Word document as you are going through the form, as if you lose your connection, all answers will be deleted.

  4. Due to people using AI to fill in this form, we have disabled copy-paste for all of the questions. Please instead write your answers here then write them again in your Word document.

  5. Your data is secure. Well, apart from the fact that our password system was considered weak even in the 90s, and there’s probably well over 1000 third party JavaScript libararies loaded on this site. Have we checked a single one of them? Nope.
